SPARTA mail list

The SPARTA mail list is hosted at SourceForge. The mail list began in Aug 2018. SPARTA developers try to respond to posted questions in a timely manner.

  • post to list by sending email to sparta-users at lists.sourceforge.net (no subscription required)
  • SPARTA home page on SourceForge
  • SPARTA mail list home page on SourceForge
  • SPARTA mail list archives on SourceForge

Ways to use the SPARTA mail list

  • Anyone can post messages to the list by sending an email to sparta-users at lists.sourceforge.net.
  • Anyone can browse/search previous questions/answers in the archives.
  • You do not have to subscribe to the list to post Qs, receive answers (to your Qs), or browse/search the archives.
  • You do need to subscribe to the list if you want emails for all the posts (per-message or in digest form), or to answer Qs yourself. Feel free to sign up and help us out!
  • You can subscribe/unsubscribe, change your subscriber options, ofview the archives from the SPARTA mail list home page.
  • If you post a message and you are a subscriber, your message will appear immediately. If you are not a subscriber, your message will be moderated, which typically takes a day. Either way, when someone replies you will receive that reply as a personal email, and the reply will appear on the list in the new thread your message started. Posting a message sends email to subscribers immediately, but it can take a few hours for postings and replies to show up in the SourceForge archive.
